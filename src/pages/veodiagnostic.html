Introduction: Diagnostic Data

Welcome to the VEO Diagnostic Data portal. This page provides access to curated datasets that support detection and monitoring of pathogens using molecular assays such as PCR, antigen testing, and sequencing-based approaches. The datasets include clinical and environmental measurements, including viral RNA in wastewater and mutation tracking relevant for diagnostic assay performance. These resources help researchers and public health professionals evaluate diagnostic reliability, identify emerging variants, and strengthen infectious disease surveillance.
