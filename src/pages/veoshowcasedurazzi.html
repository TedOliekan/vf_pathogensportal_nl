Global Urban Wastewater Virome Surveillance Dataset

Koopmans & Aarestrup + VEO consortium partners

This VEO-linked global wastewater metagenomics dataset integrates longitudinal sewage sampling from multiple cities across Europe and other regions. It focuses on untargeted virome profiling, enabling the detection of known and novel viral sequences in urban wastewater systems. By combining environmental sequencing data with spatial and temporal metadata, the dataset supports early warning surveillance, pathogen discovery, and comparative virome ecology across geographic regions. It extends traditional targeted pathogen monitoring toward ecosystem-level infectious disease surveillance.
