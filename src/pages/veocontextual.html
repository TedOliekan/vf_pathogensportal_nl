Introduction: Contextual Data

This page provides access to contextual datasets that complement genomic and diagnostic data within the VEO project. These resources capture social behavior, symptom reporting, and integrated surveillance signals, offering additional insight into how infectious diseases spread and are perceived in populations. By combining epidemiological, environmental, and social data streams, these datasets support a more comprehensive understanding of outbreak dynamics and public health responses.
