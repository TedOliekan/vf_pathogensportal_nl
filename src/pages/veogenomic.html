Introduction: Genomic Data

Welcome to the VEO Genomic Data portal. This page provides access to curated sequencing datasets within the VEO framework. These include genomic, metagenomic, and environmental sequencing data supporting research into pathogen evolution, microbial ecology, and infectious disease dynamics.
