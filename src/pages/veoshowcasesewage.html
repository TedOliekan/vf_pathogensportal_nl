European Sewage Metagenomics Surveillance Resource (Five-city One Health dataset)

Koopmans & Aarestrup + VEO consortium partners

This integrated VEO-linked One Health dataset combines longitudinal sewage surveillance from five European cities with high-resolution metagenomic profiling. It includes microbial community composition, pathogen detection, antimicrobial resistance (AMR) gene abundance, and metagenome-assembled genomes (MAGs), all linked to spatial and temporal metadata. The dataset provides a unique framework for monitoring population-level microbial dynamics and antimicrobial resistance trends using wastewater as a proxy for human and environmental health.
