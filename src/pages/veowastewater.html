Introduction: Wastewater Surveillance Data

This page provides access to comprehensive wastewater surveillance datasets from multiple European sites and global projects. These datasets include metagenomic sequencing, viral capture, and longitudinal monitoring of pathogens such as SARS-CoV-2 in sewage systems. Wastewater surveillance is a powerful tool for tracking community-level infections, detecting emerging pathogens, and assessing viral diversity in the environment, supporting timely public health responses.
