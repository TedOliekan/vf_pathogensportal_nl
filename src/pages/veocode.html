Introduction: Code

This page provides access to code, models, portals and software tools, pipelines, and databases developed within the VEO project. These resources support genomic analysis, wastewater surveillance, epidemiological modelling, and digital data integration. Together, they enable researchers to process large-scale datasets, explore pathogen evolution, and develop data-driven approaches for infectious disease surveillance and preparedness.
