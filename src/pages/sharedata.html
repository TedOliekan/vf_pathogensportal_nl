Share data

We encourage the Dutch research community to share their data in line with the FAIR1 Data Principles, to promote Findability, Accessibility, Interoperability, and Reusability of research data. You can do this by depositing your research data with publicly accessible digital repositories that are part of evaluation-based initiatives and accreditation processes2,3. This portal helps you identify recommended databases for depositing your data, ensuring compliance with best practices in data sharing.
